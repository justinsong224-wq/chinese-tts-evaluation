Voice Quality Evaluation · 2025

Comparison: Chinese TTS Systems vs. Human Voice

We picked one Chinese text that has erhua (r-coloring), numbers, long pauses, and modal particles. We compared 4 popular TTS engines with a real human voice. We want to see the differences in how natural they sound, their rhythm, and emotion. This helps us understand what AI voices can and cannot do right now.

Test Text

今儿个天气不错，您要是有空的话，咱们可以去公园走走。 2025年以来，人工智能技术发展得越来越快， 但很多人还是觉得，机器说话嘛，总归少了点儿人情味。 您说是不是这个理儿？

Core Findings
Erhua is the biggest weakness for current TTS
In a word.the pronunciation of "儿" is one of the biggest weaknesses for current TTS.Maybe because it is a conjunction,and it is not easy to pronounce correctly.
Styles don't match the situation well
The common shortcoming of current TTS is that their voice are full of machine-like feeling,and it is hard to let they consider the emotion of the words and sentences.
Emotions in particles still need human data
Modal particles (嘛, 呢, 啊) carry a lot of emotion. TTS usually can't catch the context. High-qualityhuman voice data is the key to fixing this.
